About Me

I am a research intern in machine learning at Google X and an undergraduate researcher in the Theory Group at USC, previously advised by Jason D. Lee and David Kempe. Previously, I was a research intern in machine learning at Sandia National Laboratories and the Air Force Research Laboratory. I am a junior at USC pursuing a BS in Applied and Computational Mathematics, with a minor in Computer Science. I will graduate in May 2021, and I plan to attend a PhD program in machine learning theory.

My research interests include:

  • Mathematical Foundations of Machine Learning and Data Science

  • Theory of Deep Learning and Deep Reinforcement Learning

  • Non-Convex Optimization and High-Dimensional Statistics

  • Explainability, Fairness, Robustness, and Scalability of Machine Learning

  • Randomized and Approximation Algorithms
