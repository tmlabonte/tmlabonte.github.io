About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Machine Learning at Georgia Tech advised by Tuo Zhao and an ML research intern at Microsoft Research advised by Neel Joshi. My work is generously supported by the DoD NDSEG Fellowship.

I received my B.S. in Applied and Computational Mathematics at the University of Southern California advised by Shaddin Dughmi, where I was a Trustee Scholar and Viterbi Fellow. My senior thesis in convex optimization received the Discovery Scholar distinction for exemplary research. Previously, I was an ML research intern at Google X and Sandia National Laboratories.
